Main Services

SEA FREIGHT SERVICE

If low costs matter for your shipment, try our sea freight services. concern or time is not a factor and your shipments are large and heavy you will benefit from our sea freight services. We ship significant volumes every year, which enables us to offer you capacity, frequent departures and competitive rates. We also offer a vast choice of destinations from every major port in the world across both intercontinental and short sea routes to accommodate your needs. Delivery to your doorstep our employees are ready to guide you through the complex port layouts, regulations and cultural norms to ship your cargo from port to port. And if you want pickup or delivery to your doorstep, we can handle that too. We will combine our sea freight services with our air, rail, truck and barge solutions to match your requirements and transit times. Your type of cargo Whether you have hazardous cargo, out-of-gauge freight or plain standard shipments or you need us to handle your buyer's consolidation services, our network is there to support you. • 20ft/40ft container shipping, FCL and LCL (full and less than container load) • Port to port & door to door • Dangerous good handling • Export documentation, packing & labeling • Customs clearance • Direct transshipment • Inland cartage

AIR FREIGHT SERVICE

Our staff is well trained and experienced professionals. We connected electronically to most of airlines, the air cargo terminal & Customs office through EDI. Which provides efficient booking, real time status inquiry & tracing for your shipments? We have reputable partners around the world to represent us & to take care of your cargo upon arrival. We know how to serve you in the best possible way. With our worldwide network of partners, covering more than 85 agents covering most parts of the world, ASL is offering comprehensive air freight services, as well as group age services with all Europe, Asia and North America. Import: Our Import freight professionals offer a wide range of import airfreight services, including Customs clearance, de-consolidation and delivery to door anywhere in Egypt, via our airport office. Customers requiring a full warehousing service can have their cargo stored at any of our modern fully secured warehouses and orders can be dispatched on demand to multiple destinations either locally or nationwide. Export : Our team - all experienced & professional personnel - will advise and assist with all aspects of airfreight forwarding consulting , including packing, insurance, aviation security, hazardous goods regulations and all necessary paperwork, including consular documentation With the continuing growth in airfreight as a method of global distribution, ASL ability to provide reliable and cost effective services had made it one of the Egyptian leading freight forwarders.

OVERLAND TRANSPORT

ASL’s overland transportation services are customized to deliver reliable and on-time solutions – every time. We offer a comprehensive Door-to-Door or Door-to-Port Road Haulage service to cater to our customers’ distribution needs, whilst taking care of all the tedious customs and border formalities giving our customers peace of mind. We will provide you with the best advice and complete information on the various documentation requirements at each border/point of entry in to/out of any of the GCC countries as well as overseas destinations, offering our customers a one-stop-shop for cost efficient overland transport solutions. Our current fleet consist of: 20ft and 40ft trailers to transport containers in and out of port Heavy-Duty pick-ups to transport less than container loads or sizeable weight/volume shipments Vehicle carriers to transport vehicles to port and our loading yard Low-loaders / Low-bed trailers to transport grounded heavy equipment Light-Duty pick- up vehicles to transport shipments within the city, from and to Free Zones, and Air/Sea Ports Small Pick-ups for the transport of smaller consignment of goods

CLEARANCE SERVICES

Customs clearance forms an essential part of the time-critical transport and logistics industries globally. To support our customers, our network of global customs experts, with local expertise of their respective countries play a crucial part in the seamless processes of our customers supply chains world-wide.. Customs processes are even more critical in the EGYPT. given the ideal geographical location that positions the country as a transshipment hub in the middle of many trade-lanes. This not only supports commerce in general, but the transport and logistics industries which form a crucial part of the economy. ASL is a fully licensed Customs Broker in the EGYPT. represented at all Air, Sea, and Land Border points; geared to offer various types of customs clearances to its customers across various industry segments. Our fully fledged team of well-trained and experienced staff are competent in all local regulations pertaining to imports and exports of all types of goods from various parts of the world in to the and out of the EGYPT. From straight-forward import and export clearances to multi-model shipment clearances, our experts can provide the best advice at the right time to meet our customer’s strict delivery and clearance schedules.
